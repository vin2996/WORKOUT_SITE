Nutrition Tips

Fuel your fitness journey with these basic nutrition tips:

  • 🍎 Eat more whole foods like fruits and vegetables.
  • 💧 Stay hydrated — drink at least 8 glasses of water a day.
  • 🥩 Get enough protein to support muscle growth and repair.
  • 🍚 Balance carbs, protein, and healthy fats.
  • 🍽️ Avoid skipping meals — especially breakfast.
  • ❌ Limit processed sugar and salty snacks.
  • ✅ Plan your meals ahead to avoid impulsive eating.
